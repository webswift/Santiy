Sanity OS- Appointment Setting & Follow-ups

During any lead generation or business development campaign it's highly likely you will need to schedule follow-up calls or set appointments for your sales team. Thanks to the smart and logical environment in Sanity OS booking and actioning appointments and follow-ups is a piece of cake!

Follow-ups

Follow-ups are an important part of day-to-day business. Remembering who you've got to call back is just one aspect of your day , it's likely you and your team probably have a thousand other things going on. Sanity OS has a built in follow-up diary with reminders to alert you when scheduled tasks are nearby. You can book them in just a few clicks and view them in a dedicated follow-up section. That's not all, Sanity OS can seamlessly send you and/or your prospect an email with an invite to sync a follow-up to any external calendar.

Appointment Setting

A common problem with appointment setting is that the person who is arranging the date and time of an appointment may be doing it on behalf of a third party or a team member without CRM access. Sanity OS lets you add as many Sales members to your account as you like so when an appointment is booked for them they receive a document containing all the information about the opportunity along with a date, time and Sync to Calendar request.

In Sanity OS a sales member is an individual that doesn't use or have access to the system and leans on you and your team to populate their appointment diary. They can be added in just a few clicks and booking appointments or viewing their calendar is just as easy.
