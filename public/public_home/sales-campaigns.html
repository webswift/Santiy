Sanity OS- What are Campaigns?

A sales campaign is a planned sales strategy that typically includes a list of leads and associated contact information. Using Sanity OS you can create unlimited campaigns, import your leads and set particular preferences to match your sales strategy.

More than just segmenting Data

In Sanity OS creating a campaign is about more than just segmenting data, reports and user permissions. When you launch a new sales campaign you also have additional options that specify a custom lead form layout, mailsever preferences, qualification criertia and webforms.

If this all sounds great but too complicated think again! You can setup a campaign with as many or as little options as you like in just a few minutes with just a few clicks.
